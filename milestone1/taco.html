Tacos al Pastor

  • Tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa verde
  • Onion
  • Seasoned Pork
  • Lime
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Begin by perparing all of the condiments for the taco.
  2. This includes: cutting up limes, onions, and cilantro.
  3. Season meat as perferred.
  4. Continue by placing a thin layer of oil to a pan on a stove top.
  5. Let the oil heat up and continue by placing the meat on the pan.
  6. Stir meat and continue to heat until it reaches a brown color
  7. Afterwards, you will want to set a dash of oil onto a griddle.
  8. Continue by cooking tortillas for approximately 1 minute. Make sure to flip them aswell.
  9. Finally, place your cooked meat onto the tortillas and add all the cut up condiments that were previously prepared and and salsa verde.
