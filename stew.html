Quesadilla

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Out of all the foods I have included so far, this will be the easiest by far.
  2. Obtain a cheese of your choosing, but monterrey jack or cheddar are perferred
  3. Place your tortilla on a griddle and begin to let it heat up for about 20 seconds.
  4. Now flip your tortilla and add cheese.
  5. Fold the tortilla into a taco shape and cook until the cheese melts
